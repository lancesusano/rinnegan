Balcony

           The balcony is literally the hardest area to clean inside the unit. It does not possess a drainage system which allows the water to tower inches above my foot on a really stormy day. If it wasn't for the sliding door, the unit would've been a lot harder to clean. The balcony also functions as a chilling area for my friends and I, putting us all in a profound zone without the use of alcohol - yielding a very productive discourse between social issues and various life phenomena. The scenery, the beautiful sunset, and the night lights are all factors which constitute to the said being. Philosophy, politics, and moral choices are primarily the usual topics of interests in the conversations. Such conversations made me realize that life truly is beautiful; quite of difficulty, yet boldly beautiful.
