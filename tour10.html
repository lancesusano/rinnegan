Kitchen

           Whenever I'm in the condominium, my diet circulates around fast food, cheap pizza, and salty chips. That is, of course, whenever I'm accompanied by someone. There is not, by any chance, am I going to eat alone inside the unit. I've tried it once though, and I did not like the feeling. It made me feel like I was resented by many, which accounts for the reason why I'm eating alone. Anyway, even when I'm eating outside, my diet remains the same; fast food, cheap pizza, and salty chips. I'm a very cheap boy fond of the quotation "Mahal eh. Pwede na siguro 'to." Trust me when I tell you this: I wouldn't spend a dime on burger king if it's only going to be me who's eating a burger.
