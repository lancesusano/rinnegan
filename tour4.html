Unit

           The unit means a lot to me, not just because of the personal experiences that I've shared with it, but also because of the familial episodes that have occured upon it. The unit has been around ever since i was, i think, 9. The unit was purchased solely for our relatives who traveled back and forth from the States to the Philippines. As time progressed, they've decided to settle in different states like New York, New Jersey, and Los Angeles, with their respective families. Since nobody is occupying the said unit, I've decided to man it for myself; with hopes of destroying the notion of wasting money and to maintain cleanliness and elegance intact.
