Bedroom and the Comfort Room

           The bedroom and the comfort room are both of my favorite rooms specifically because they both connote rest. Whenever you're in the bathroom, you are not expected to do anything else but to relax. Moreover, you can utilize the space to enhance your performing skills like dancing and singing. The bedroom, on the otherside of the bronx, is quite self-explanatory. Who wouldn't want to rest inside one's bedroom? In my current stage in life, as a college student, rest is a practice that i rarely indulge myself into. So whenever given the chance, rest assured I'll take it!
