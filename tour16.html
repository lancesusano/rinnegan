Bedroom

           The bedroom is a private area where you can do whatever you want to do without restrictions and attending to the expectations of others. Of course, since I'm mostly alone in the unit, the freedom to act isn't normally an issue of concern. But it's a completely different story whenever I'm with other people inside the said space. Since my friends differ from me in the sense that they enjoy the living room more than the bedroom, I often contain myself inside the room whenever urged by feelings of being alone. Worry not! This is not an anti-social tendency; it's merely a part of my personality. Fortunately, my friends know how to recognize other people's individual needs - probably the greatest cognitive feature possessed by my friends that astonishes me dearly.
