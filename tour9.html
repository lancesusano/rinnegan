Living Room

           The contents of the living room is multi-functional. The living room table serves as an extra kitchen table whenever there's too much people occupying the unit. Moreover, the wooden sofa serves as an extra bed for my friends and I. The bed can shelter three sleeping person, and that's about it. Okay, perhaps four when we're persistent enough to try. The thing is, nobody wants to sleep in the living room primarily because of the possibility of a figure appearing in the balcony. In the condominium, every night is a test of friendship. To what extent can your hospitality reach?
