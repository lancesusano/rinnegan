Vivant Flats

           To be frank, I momentarily stay in Vivant Flats. The only reason why I decided to have the condominium as the primary place in the tour section of this web page is because of the fun memories that I hold onto it. My unit resides in the second floor of the condominium, particularly in room number 10. The entire condominium lies in Brgy. Alabang, Muntinlupa city - my hometown. Staying in Vivant Flats has its benefits; but among the rest, I enjoy the freedom of being alone. Of course, all benefits have contradictory downsides; well in this case, you are of your own service - cleaning the entire condominium alone, cooking your own food, and washing your own clothes.
