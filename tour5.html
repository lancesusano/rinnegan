Television

           In all honestly, I rarely watch television; But when I do, I make sure that it's either HBO, Jack TV, Fox, or Cartoon Network. American sitcoms astonish me dearly; and it's not because of the colonial mentality in me, but because of their colorful contemporary lifestyle. Applying tv shows to the Philippine context, it's always either about love or fantasy. It's never about the everyday life of individuals and the way they fare with the challenges of the world. This theme, to me, appears to be more relatable compared to what we are used to. Fortunately today, in the Philippines, certain Indie films with unique plotlines are starting to surface - like Barboys and Birdshot . I do hope that more different films elevate in the imminent years to come - you know, to redefine the stigma that accompanies contemporary Philippine film industry.
