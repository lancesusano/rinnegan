Hallway

           If there's one thing that I hate about the condominium, it's definitely the hallway. Nothing specific really, just the entire design of the hallway. You see, there's something about the way I walk that makes a lot of noise. And this isn't something that I intentionally do; albeit, something that comes out naturally. I remember having to rush inside the unit because my stomach is acting up. Basically, I was running - when this person, of japanese descent, reprimanded me in a harsh tone. In all honesty, I really wouldn't blame him. Even I would tell my heel to land properly and be quiet. Ever since then, I've decided to be extra careful when walking the said path; but the issue lies within this line: "I always forget."
